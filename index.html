Andy Choi

Undergraduate student at Carnegie Mellon Univeristy, majoring in electrical and computer engineering, minoring in computer science, minoring in business administration, and graduating in Spring 2015.

Currently interested in artificial intelligence, machine learning, web technology, and human factors.
